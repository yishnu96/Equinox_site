About TIB

Techno International Batanagar is a dynamic endeavour of Techno India Group Techno India Group is the largest education group in Eastern India and one of the largest in the country.

During its voyage, Techno India Group has strengthened its original
emphasis Science & Engineering, thus creating potential enhancement
in education.

Thus, being one of the representatives of this group, Techno International Batanagar seeks to optimize distincted sophistication and integrity in the academic field.

About Equinox

The Annual Technical and Cultural Fest of Techno International
Batanagar, EQUINOX is one of the most progressing and evolving
fest across Kolkata. We, the students are set for arranging the 5th season of EQUINOX this year. It has been a platform for a lot of students to
portray their talents, and has also been a stage for many popular and
growing entertaining performers and groups like Underground Authority, The Radical Array project (T.R.A.P.), Delete and Krosswindz. Dedicated to
the celebration of technology and creativity, Equinox promises to be an unforgettable extravaganza altogether.
