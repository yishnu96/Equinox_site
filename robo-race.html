Width of track is about 2.5cm to 3cm. 5minutes will be given to the team for dry run after which the team has to start the final run. The Event will be a tournament following league system for the qualifiers. Two teams will race simultaneously on parallel tracks. The team which finishes the track fast will be awarded 3 points. In case of a tie 1 point will be awarded to each team. 4 Teams will qualify for the semi-finals based on points, any ties will be resolved using average time to finish the track.

Lego parts will not be allowed.
Any kind of damage to arena will lead to direct disqualification.
